Deep Sea Fishing

Deep sea fishing is an aggressive hobby that takes dedication, toughness, and alot of energy.

Equiptment

Offshore fishing is quite an expensive hobby so you'll need a bit of eiptment:

-First you'll need your vessel: Preferably larger than 23 ft

-Second You'll need your fishing rods and or spears depending on what fish you are trying to catch

-Third on the list will be safety equiptment: Life vest, flare gun, channel radio, and even a life vessel if possible.

-Fourth will be food and drinks: Depending on how long you plan to stay out, sandwiches are always solid options or pizza in ziplock bags

-Fifth on the list is fuel: Before ever single trip, you will want to completely fill your tank on gas

-Sixth and last on the list is ice: You can never have enough Ice. Keep them fish and drinks cold or you'll be in a bind.
